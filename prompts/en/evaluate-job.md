# Job-fit evaluation

Act as a careful career advisor. Compare the candidate profile and job posting below.

Return:
1. A 0-100 fit score with a brief justification.
2. Required skills that match, with evidence from the profile.
3. Requirements that are missing or uncertain.
4. Seniority, location, language, salary, and remote-work compatibility.
5. Scam or employment-risk signals.
6. A recommendation: apply, apply selectively, learn first, or skip.
7. Three truthful CV adjustments for this role.

Rules:
- Do not invent experience, metrics, certifications, dates, or tools.
- Separate required qualifications from preferred qualifications.
- Treat missing information as unknown, not as a match.
- Explain assumptions.
